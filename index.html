Hi, I'm Will.

I am a PhD student in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Pennsylvania. I completed my MA in Linguistic Studies at Syracuse University, where I was advised by Dr. Jaklin Kornfilt. My thesis used NLP methods to compare elicitations and natural usage of focus and complementation structures in Guadeloupean Creole, using data collected during NSF-sponsored fieldwork.

My research spans computational linguistics, French-based Creoles, syntax, and experimental methods. I'm interested in uses of NLP for documentation, bilingual education, and collaborative experimental linguistics.
