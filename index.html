Hi, I’m Will.

I am a second-year MA student in linguistic studies, with concentrations in NLP and syntactic theory, advised by Dr. Jaklin Kornfilt. My thesis uses NLP methods to compare elicitations and natural usage of focus and complementation structures in Guadeloupean Creole, using data collected during NSF-sponsored fieldwork.

My research spans computational linguistics, French-based Creoles, syntax, and experimental methods. I'm interested in NLP for documentation, bilingual education, and collaborative experimental linguistics.
